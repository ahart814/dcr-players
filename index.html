Update SimplerSport

SimplerSport is a tool for tracking player availability for practices and games. It's incredibly important for our coaches. Please keep your SimplerSport availability up to date at all times (including any last-minute schedule changes), and use the "add note" feature to include any additional information (you're going to be late, you're not able to do contact, etc).
